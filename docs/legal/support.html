AuraLunis Support

Subscriptions

Manage or cancel: iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → AuraLunis.

Restore Purchases

AuraLunis → Settings → Manage Plan → Restore Purchases.

Sky Lens

Enable Camera and Motion in iPhone Settings → AuraLunis. Try calibration in Settings → Sky Lens.

Location

Enable While Using location in iPhone Settings → AuraLunis.
