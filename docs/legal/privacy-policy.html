AuraLunis — Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

Location (coarse). Approximate location for planet positions, weather, Tonight Score. Processed on-device. City-level sent to OpenWeatherMap for weather only.

Purchase history. RevenueCat processes subscriptions via Apple StoreKit. We receive subscription status and anonymous ID. Never see payment details.

Device identifier. RevenueCat anonymous ID only. No IDFA.

Usage analytics. Paywall views, session counts. Processed locally. No third-party analytics.

AI Sky Companion (optional). Your sky question + sky context sent to Anthropic API. No personal info sent.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Name, email, phone, contacts, photos, health data, browsing history, precise GPS, biometrics.

3. Cosmic Vault

Encrypted on-device with NaCl secretbox. Key in iOS Keychain. Never leaves your device. Never transmitted.

4. Camera and Sensors

Sky Lens uses camera and motion sensors on-device only. Never recorded or transmitted.

5. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (subscriptions), OpenWeatherMap (weather), Anthropic (AI, optional).

6. Your Rights

Delete all data by uninstalling. Manage subscriptions in Apple Settings. Contact: manager@ocoeestudios.com
