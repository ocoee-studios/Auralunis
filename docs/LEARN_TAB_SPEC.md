# Chronaura Learn Tab Specification

## Purpose
The Learn tab is the educational heart of Chronaura. It explains planets, constellations, stars, the Moon, the Solar System, Deep Sky objects, nebulae, galaxies, and the Milky Way in a beginner-friendly way.

## Why it deserves its own tab
Explore is for pointing, finding, and browsing the live sky. Learn is for understanding what the user is seeing.

## Learn categories
- Solar System
- Moon
- Planets
- Constellations
- Stars
- Deep Sky
- Milky Way / Galaxy Mode
- Learn the Sky in 30 Nights

## MVP lessons
- What is the Solar System?
- Moon Phases
- Inner vs Outer Planets
- What are Constellations?
- Why are some stars brighter?
- What are Nebulae?
- The Milky Way Band
- Night 1: Find the Moon

## Production behavior
Each lesson should connect to:
- Sky Lens
- Celestial Archive
- Save to Vault
- Listen / audio explanation
- Beginner quiz
- Guided sky challenge

## Tone
Educational, beautiful, beginner-friendly, science-forward, and not overwhelming.
