## Visual rules

Preserve the exact design language shown in the approved board:

- Midnight navy / near-black background
- warm astral-gold linework
- crescent-C astrolabe emblem
- three-ring celestial geometry
- compass-style axis lines
- central starburst
- small orbital accents
- refined serif AuraLunis wordmark
- slogan: `YOUR TIME, WRITTEN IN THE STARS.`
- descriptor: `YOUR SKY. YOUR STORY.`
- gold sparkle field and restrained cosmic texture

## In-app use

The approved emblem should appear consistently in:

- top app lockup
- Home astrolabe experience
- Sky Lens header
- Watch companion screen
- Learn header
- Settings header
- app icon
- watchOS astrolabe face
- Watch complications / Smart Stack artwork where appropriate

## Do not do this

- Do not replace the emblem with a generic crescent moon.
- Do not use a plain compass icon as the main logo.
- Do not simplify away the crescent-C shape.
- Do not redraw the brand in a different style.
- Do not create a new logo direction unless explicitly requested.

The existing drawn placeholders in the React Native repo may remain temporarily only until the production SVGs are recreated from this approved board.
