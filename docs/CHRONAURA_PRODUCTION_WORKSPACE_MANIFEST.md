## Crown-jewel future modules

### Aura Ambient Spatial Audio Room Casting
A future premium ambient layer for multi-room spatial sound. This should never override home audio without explicit consent. MVP fallback should be local-device-only sound bath.

### Apple Vision Pro Stellar Portal
A future visionOS target that uses spatial anchors and passthrough to transform a room ceiling or wall into a private cosmic observatory.

### Sovereign Sigil Secure Widget Customization
A future widget personalization system that creates a unique vector sigil from hashed/local-safe user data. Do not expose raw birth data or device identifiers in widget files.
