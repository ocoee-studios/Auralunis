## 1. Aura Ambient Spatial Audio Room Casting

### Vision
Turn a physical room into a living sound environment that responds to celestial alignments.

### MVP-safe version
- Local-device sound bath
- Optional AirPlay handoff
- User manually starts playback
- No automatic override of existing music

### Future version
- Multi-room speaker routing
- Spatial sweeps across room zones
- Alignment-triggered ambient events
- Room-based sound choreography

### Safety / platform rules
- Do not override active home audio automatically.
- Require clear user consent.
- Respect Focus modes, Do Not Disturb, quiet hours, and volume limits.
- Provide instant stop.
